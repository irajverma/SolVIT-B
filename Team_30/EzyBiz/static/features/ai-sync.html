How It Works

The Technology Behind AI Sync

Our cutting-edge AI technology powers the seamless synchronization between your offline and online sales channels.

Invoice & Document Processing

EzyBiz uses advanced OCR and Natural Language Processing to automatically extract product information from invoices, purchase orders, and other documents.

  • Automatic data extraction from PDFs and scanned documents
  • Recognition of product codes, prices, quantities, and descriptions
  • Smart matching with existing inventory database

Data Transformation & Normalization

Our AI engine standardizes data from different sources to ensure consistency across all channels, handling variations in formats and measurements.

  • Conversion between different units and measurement systems
  • Normalization of product attributes and categories
  • Creation of unified product profiles

Real-time Synchronization Engine

The core synchronization engine ensures that all your sales channels are updated whenever changes occur in any connected system.

  • Event-based synchronization with millisecond latency
  • Two-way data flow between offline and online channels
  • Scheduled batch updates for efficiency

Conflict Resolution AI

When conflicting information is detected across different channels, our AI automatically resolves discrepancies based on configurable business rules and learning patterns.

  • Intelligent conflict detection across channels
  • Rule-based and AI-driven resolution strategies
  • Continuous learning from manual resolutions
